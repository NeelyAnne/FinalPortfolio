About Me

Welcome to my page! I was born in Augusta, Georgia and lived in Waynesboro, Georgia for the first 18 years of my life. My home county has a total of nine stoplights so you could say it's pretty small. After highschool I moved to Athens to attend the University of Georgia. While at UGA, I was part of many on campus organizations such as Delta Zeta, American Medical Studen Association and UGA miracle.

I received my Bachelor of Science in general biology in the spring of 2019 and have recently moved to Atlanta to attend a coding bottcamo hosted by the Georgia Institute of Technology. After this bootcamp is over, I plan to pursue a job in the development world in some aspect. I'm excited to see where this path takes me!
